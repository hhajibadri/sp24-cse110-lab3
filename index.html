Sometimes, life happens and we struggle to catch up on what we missed. With meeting notes you can easily recall meetings so you don't miss out on what's important.

A meeting note may include the following.

  • Title of the meeting
  • Date of the meeting
  • Subject of the meeting
  • ...

You may also want to prioritize certain things when writing a meeting note

  1. Title and date of the meeting
  2. Important topics discussed
  3. Upcoming tasks and deadlines
  4. ...
